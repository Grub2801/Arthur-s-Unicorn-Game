PORTFOLIO

Shut-the-Box

Roll the dice and lay down any numerical combination of tiles that match your roll. Just keep on rolling until you can no longer match your roll on the remaining tiles. The lowest score wins the game. If you lay down all the tiles, then you've "shut the box". A simple game built using HTML, CSS and JavaScript with jQuery.
